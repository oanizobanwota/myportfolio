About Me

Welcome!

Hello, I'm Onyebuchi Anizoba-Nwota. Currently, I serve as the Managing Director of FreshSHOP Supermarket, a retail outlet for groceries and household needs, my passion lies in the transformative power of technology. With a Bachelor's degree in Educational Management & Policy, I've recently embarked on a journey into full-stack development. Through a comprehensive program with Learning People Global, I've gained proficiency in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and React, along with practical experience in Python, SQL, and Git/GitHub for version control. Driven by the desire to create technological solutions that benefit humanity, I'm excited to apply my new skills. I'm also a proud member of the British Computer Society (BCS).
